# TWEWY Arcade Game
A TWEWY fangame made to the best of my ability.
A passion project.

This game is being made for about 6 months (starting [March 31st, 2026]).

The game is far from complete.
Missing:
-Enemies
-Top screen
-Pins
-Main menu (No pause menu)
-Scoreboard
-Credits

I will not accept pull requests until the 6 months are done.
Why?
I want every bit of this to show my skill in different aspects of the game and show it off at a science fair this year [2026].
Having others help means it's no longer just me and therefore I can no longer say "I did something" beacause, in reality, I probably took help from someone better than me.
It blends the line of my skill as an individual and my skill as a group.
I don't want to depend on others to do the work for me and I want to improve my own game making skills.

I will check for issues and bugs people may find and after the 6 months I will start taking help in code (mostly), art (in the OG Ds style), music (loopable versions), etc. to make the game as good as it can be. No guarantees but I will try.
